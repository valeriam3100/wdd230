Ovar is a Portuguese city located in the Aveiro district, Centro Region and inter-municipal community of Aveiro, with about 17,200 inhabitants.

Pena Palace

It was one of the main residences of the Portuguese royal family during the 19th century.

Cabo da Roca

It is the cape located at the westernmost point of continental Portugal and, therefore, of the Iberian Peninsula and of continental Europe.

Caldas da Rainha

It is part of the "Turismo do Centro".
