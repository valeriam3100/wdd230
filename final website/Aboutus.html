Our History

Fresh Fruit Drinks was founded in 2001 by two friends who were passionate about fresh, healthy food. The company started out as a small juice bar, but it quickly grew in popularity. Today, Fresh Fruit Drinks is a leading provider of fresh fruit drinks and smoothies.

Our mission is to provide our customers with the freshest, most delicious fruit drinks possible. We use only the highest quality fruits, and we blend our drinks to order. We also offer a variety of customization options, so our customers can create their own perfect drink.

About the Area

Fresh Fruit Drinks is located in Carlsbad, California. Carlsbad is a beautiful coastal city with a mild climate. It's known for its beaches, its golf courses, and its many parks.

Carlsbad is also home to a number of other businesses and organizations that are passionate about healthy living. This makes it a great place for Fresh Fruit Drinks to do business.
